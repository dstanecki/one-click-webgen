Fun Facts About Bunnies

Fact 1: Bunnies are not rodents

Despite popular belief, rabbits are not rodents. They belong to the family Leporidae and are actually lagomorphs, along with pikas and hares.

Fact 2: Bunnies have almost 360-degree vision

Rabbits have a nearly 360-degree vision, which allows them to detect predators from all directions. They can see everything behind them and only have a small blind spot in front of their nose.

Fact 3: Bunnies can't vomit

Unlike cats and dogs, rabbits can't vomit. This is why it's crucial for them to have a diet that's high in fiber to keep their digestive system moving smoothly.
