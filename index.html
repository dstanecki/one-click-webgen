10 Obscure Fun Facts About Ducks

Fact 1

Ducks are found on every continent except Antarctica.

Fact 2

Some species of ducks can live up to 20 years.

Fact 3

Ducks have excellent vision, they can see in full color and have a field of vision of nearly 340 degrees.

Fact 4

Ducks have three eyelids.

Fact 5

The feathers of ducks are waterproof.

Fact 6

Ducks have been domesticated as pets and farm animals for more than 500 years.

Fact 7

All domestic ducks are descended from either the Mallard or the Muscovy duck.

Fact 8

Ducks are omnivores.

Fact 9

The "quack" of a duck does echo.

Fact 10

Ducks like to sleep with one eye open.
