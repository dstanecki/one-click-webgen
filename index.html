Upcoming Lexus LFR

The transmission of the Lexus LFR is expected to be an automatic, possibly an 8-speed or a 10-speed. This would provide smooth and efficient gear changes, enhancing the driving experience.

The Lexus LFR, with its high-performance engine and advanced transmission, is expected to deliver impressive performance. It is speculated to have a top speed in excess of 200 mph and an acceleration from 0 to 60 mph in less than 3 seconds.
