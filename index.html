10 Obscure Fun Facts About Ducks

Fact 1

Ducks are found on every continent except Antarctica.

Fact 2

Ducks have excellent vision, and can see in full color.

Fact 3

The feet of ducks do not have nerves or blood vessels, which means they don't feel the cold when swimming in icy water.

Fact 4

Ducks have been domesticated as pets and farm animals for more than 500 years.

Fact 5

All domestic ducks are descended from either the Mallard or the Muscovy duck.

Fact 6

Ducks have a specialized diet that allows them to eat a variety of foods, including plants, insects, and fish.

Fact 7

Ducks have a gland near their tail that produces oil, which they spread over their feathers to make them waterproof.

Fact 8

The "quack" of a duck does echo, despite a common myth to the contrary.

Fact 9

Some species of ducks can live up to 20 years.

Fact 10

The world's rarest duck is the Madagascar Pochard, with fewer than 100 individuals left in the wild.
