10 Fun Facts About Silver Marten Rabbits

Fact 1

Silver Marten rabbits were first recognized as a breed in the United States in 1924.

Fact 2

They are known for their distinctive coat color, which is a mix of white and another color, usually black, blue, chocolate, or sable.

Fact 3

Silver Marten rabbits are medium-sized, typically weighing between 6 and 9 pounds.

Fact 4

They are a popular breed for showing due to their striking appearance and friendly temperament.

Fact 5

Silver Marten rabbits have a lifespan of 5 to 8 years, but can live up to 10 years with proper care.

Fact 6

They are a very active breed and require plenty of exercise and mental stimulation.

Fact 7

Silver Marten rabbits are known to be very intelligent and can be trained to use a litter box.

Fact 8

They are a social breed and enjoy the company of other rabbits and humans.

Fact 9

Silver Marten rabbits have a high-quality fur that is dense and soft to the touch.

Fact 10

Their diet consists of hay, pellets, vegetables, water, and occasional treats.
