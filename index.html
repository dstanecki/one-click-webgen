10 Obscure Fun Facts About Ducks

Fact 1: Ducks Never Get Cold

Thanks to their waterproof feathers, ducks are able to float in water without getting cold or wet underneath.

Fact 2: Ducks Have Excellent Vision

Ducks have a field of vision of nearly 340 degrees and can see in full color.

Fact 3: Ducks Can Sleep with One Eye Open

When sleeping in a risky environment, a duck can keep one eye open to stay alert for predators.

Fact 4: Ducks Have Three Eyelids

They have an upper and lower eyelid, and a third that's found on the side of the eye. The third eyelid is clear and acts like goggles when the duck dives underwater.

Fact 5: Ducks Can Fly Quite Fast

Despite their heavy, waddling appearance, ducks are excellent fliers, with speeds up to 60 mph.

Fact 6: Ducks Are Found All Over the World

Except for Antarctica, ducks can be found on every continent.

Fact 7: Ducks Have Been Domesticated for Thousands of Years

Humans have been raising ducks for more than 500 years longer than chickens.

Fact 8: Ducks Have a Variety of Vocalizations

They don't just "quack." Ducks can whistle, grunt, squeak, and even growl.

Fact 9: Ducks Have Long Lifespans

Some species of ducks can live up to 20 years.

Fact 10: Ducks Have a Special Organ to Clean Their Feathers

It's called a preen gland, and it produces oil that ducks use to clean their feathers and keep them waterproof.
