10 Fun Facts About Ducks

Fact 1

Ducks are birds in the family Anatidae. They are related to swans and geese.

Fact 2

Ducks have a wide range of habitats including freshwater and sea water, and they can be found all over the world except for Antarctica.

Fact 3

Ducks are omnivores. They feed on aquatic plants, small fish, insects, and grasses.

Fact 4

Most species of ducks have a lifespan of 2-12 years. Some species can live up to 20 years.

Fact 5

Ducks have excellent vision. They have three eyelids and can see in color.

Fact 6

The male ducks are called drakes, the females are called hens, and the babies are called ducklings.

Fact 7

Ducks have been domesticated as pets and farm animals for more than 500 years.

Fact 8

All ducks have highly waterproof feathers as a result of an intricate feather structure and a waxy coating that is spread on each feather while preening.

Fact 9

A duck's quack does not echo, and no one knows why.

Fact 10

The "duck face" pose, popular in social media selfies, is modeled after the puckered beak of a duck.
