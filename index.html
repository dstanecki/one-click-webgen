How to Get Jacked and Lean

Introduction

Getting jacked and lean is a process that involves a combination of strength training, cardio exercises, and a balanced diet.

Strength Training

Strength training is essential for building muscle mass. It involves exercises such as weightlifting, push-ups, pull-ups, and squats.

Cardio Exercises

Cardio exercises are crucial for burning fat and improving heart health. These include running, cycling, swimming, and high-intensity interval training (HIIT).

Balanced Diet

A balanced diet is key to fueling your workouts and building muscle. It should include a mix of proteins, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.

Additional Tips

Stay hydrated, get enough sleep, and take rest days to allow your body to recover.
