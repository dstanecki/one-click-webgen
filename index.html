10 Obscure Fun Facts About Ducks

Fact 1

Ducks are found on every continent except for Antarctica.

Fact 2

The word 'duck' comes from the Old English 'duce' meaning 'diver'.

Fact 3

A baby duck is called a duckling, and an adult male is a drake.

Fact 4

Ducks have three eyelids.

Fact 5

The eyes of a duck are located on the sides of its head, allowing it to see in nearly every direction at once.

Fact 6

Ducks have an excellent sense of vision and can even see in color.

Fact 7

Ducks are omnivores, which means they eat both plants and other animals.

Fact 8

The feathers of ducks are waterproof due to an intricate feather structure and a waxy coating that is spread on each feather while preening.

Fact 9

A group of ducks can be called a raft, team, or paddling.

Fact 10

Some species of ducks can live up to 20 years.
