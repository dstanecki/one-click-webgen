10 Fun Facts About Ducks

Fact 1

Ducks are birds in the family Anatidae. They are related to swans and geese.

Fact 2

The word 'duck' comes from the Old English 'duce' meaning 'diver'.

Fact 3

Ducks have a special layer of waterproof feathers, with the outer feathers shedding water and the underneath feathers staying completely dry.

Fact 4

Some species of ducks can live up to 20 years.

Fact 5

Ducks have excellent vision. They have three eyelids and can see in color.

Fact 6

Ducks are found on every continent except Antarctica.

Fact 7

Most species of ducks have a wide range of calls, from whistles to coos to yodels.

Fact 8

Ducks are omnivorous and will eat grass, aquatic plants, insects, seeds, fruit, fish, and other types of food.

Fact 9

Male ducks are called drakes, females are called hens, and baby ducks are called ducklings.

Fact 10

The Mallard is the most common duck species in the world, and it is the ancestor of most domestic duck breeds.
