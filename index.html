10 Obscure Fun Facts About Ducks

Fact 1: Ducks Have Waterproof Feathers

Ducks have a special gland located near their tail that produces oil. They spread this oil onto their feathers to make them waterproof.

Fact 2: Ducks Have Excellent Vision

Ducks have a field of vision of nearly 340 degrees and can see in full color.

Fact 3: Ducks Can Live Up to 20 Years

With proper care, some duck species can live up to 20 years or more in captivity.

Fact 4: Ducks Have Been Domesticated for Thousands of Years

Ducks were domesticated by humans thousands of years ago for their eggs, meat, and feathers.

Fact 5: Ducks Don't Feel Cold in Their Feet

Ducks have a counter-current heat exchange system in their legs which prevents them from losing body heat through their feet in cold water.

Fact 6: Ducks Can Fly at High Speeds

Some duck species can fly up to 60 miles per hour.

Fact 7: Ducks Have a Variety of Vocalizations

Ducks are known for their "quack", but they also have a variety of other sounds including grunts, whistles, and coos.

Fact 8: Ducks Have a Complex Social Structure

Ducks have a complex social structure that includes a pecking order.

Fact 9: Ducks Are Found All Over the World

Ducks are found on every continent except Antarctica.

Fact 10: Ducks Are Omnivores

Ducks are omnivores and will eat a wide variety of food including plants, insects, and small fish.
