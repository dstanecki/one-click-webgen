5 Best Enthusiast Cars of the 2020s So Far

1. 2020 Chevrolet Corvette Stingray

The 2020 Chevrolet Corvette Stingray is a high-performance sports car. It features a mid-engine layout, which is a first for the Corvette family. This vehicle is powered by a 6.2L V8 engine that produces up to 495 horsepower.

2. 2020 Porsche 911 Carrera

The 2020 Porsche 911 Carrera is a luxury sports car that delivers an exceptional driving experience. It is powered by a 3.0L twin-turbocharged flat-six engine that produces up to 379 horsepower.

3. 2020 BMW M3

The 2020 BMW M3 is a high-performance sedan that offers a blend of luxury and performance. It is powered by a 3.0L twin-turbocharged inline-six engine that produces up to 473 horsepower.

4. 2020 Ford Mustang Shelby GT500

The 2020 Ford Mustang Shelby GT500 is a high-performance muscle car. It is powered by a supercharged 5.2L V8 engine that produces up to 760 horsepower, making it the most powerful street-legal Ford ever.

5. 2020 Toyota Supra

The 2020 Toyota Supra is a sports car that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine that produces up to 335 horsepower.
